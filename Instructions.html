INSTRUCTIONS

1. There are 3 Levels in the Wordle Game: Easy, Medium and Hard.

2. A Player can choose any level as per his/her interest.

3. The player will guess a word as per the specification of the range of length of the word according to the selected level.

 Range of Length of the Word that the player can guess at each level:

  Level-1: Easy: 2-5

  Level-2: Medium: 6-9

  Level-3: Hard: 10-45

4. Table-1 will be displayed with all letters A-Z in the form of a 6 × 5 matrix with column number of each letter.

5. The player should enter the total number of letters in the guessed word.

6. The player should also enter the column number of each letter from Table-1.

7. Then, Table-2 will be displayed which is a matrix of order Total Number of Letters in the Word × 6 along with the column number of each letter.

8. The player should again enter the column number of each letter from Table-2.

9. Finally, the correct word will be displayed which the player had thought at the starting of the game.

10. Click on the 'PLAY' Button to Download the Wordle Game and have fun!
